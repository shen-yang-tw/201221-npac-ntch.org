節目簡介

衛武營國家藝術文化中心開幕音樂會，由國際知名指揮家，同時為衛武營藝術總監簡文彬領軍，傳奇鋼琴鬥士劉孟捷、男高音寇比·魏爾契(C. Welch)、男中音巫白玉璽、女高音陳美玲、女中音陳珮琪及國立臺灣交響樂團、高雄市交響樂團、高雄室內合唱團，將帶來包含國人作曲家馬水龍教授與金希文教授的作品、李斯特的鋼琴協奏曲與貝多芬第九號交響曲《快樂頌》等，揭開全臺唯一葡萄園式音樂廳的神秘面紗，也為衛武營開幕季揭幕，邀您親臨感受臺灣及國際音樂群星閃耀的頂級盛會。

Led by conductor Chien Wen-pin, artistic director of National Kaohsiung Center for the Arts, Taiwan's largest theater and its first vineyard-style concert hall makes its debut with an all-star cast: organist Iveta Apkalna, pianist Liu Meng-chieh, sheng “chinese pipe” artist Chen Yi-wei, tenor Corby Welch, baritone Wu Bai Yu-His, soprano Mei-ling Chen, mezzo-soprano Pei-chi Chen, National Taiwan Symphony Orchestra, Kaohsiung City Symphony Orchestra, Kaohsiung Chamber Choir, and musicians from Kaohsiung Music Alliance. Come for this very first astounding acoustic experience at the Center’s signature Concert Hall.
